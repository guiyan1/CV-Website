Data analysis & Tableau visualization for store sales performance

The case is to integrate and analyze sales revenue across different market regions and various products. Utilizing Tableau, the data will be visually presented to enhance comprehension and insights.

  • Data Integration: Combine and clean the sales data to ensure consistency and accuracy.
  • Market Region Analysis: Segment sales data based on different market regions to understand regional sales performance.
  • Product Analysis: Analyze sales revenue for different product categories or individual products.
  • Tableau Visualization: Utilize Tableau's visualization capabilities to create interactive and insightful dashboards.
